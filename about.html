About

Here at InventMe, my mission is to create new products which allow people to enjoy their life or make it easier on themselves.
InventMe sells these products online and they are 3D printed.

The products are currently made from a type of plastic known as PLA. The printer melts the plastic through a nozzle at 195-220 degrees Celsius. This material then gets put on the print bed, which is anywhere between 55-85 degrees Celsius.
